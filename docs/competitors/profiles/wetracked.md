# wetracked.io

**URL:** https://www.wetracked.io
**Target customer:** Shopify and WooCommerce merchants running paid ads (Meta, Google, TikTok, Pinterest, Snapchat, X) who need accurate conversion tracking in the post-iOS14/ad-blocker era. SMB to mid-market DTC, typically scaling ad spend. 9,000+ active merchants claimed.
**Pricing:** Order-volume-based, monthly or annual (20% annual discount).
- **Starter** -- $49/mo ($39.20/mo annual). 500 orders/mo, 3 stores.
- **Business** -- $149/mo ($119.20/mo annual). 3,000 orders/mo, 5 stores. Most popular.
- **Scale-up** -- $249/mo ($199.20/mo annual). 7,500 orders/mo, 10 stores.
- **Enterprise** -- custom. 7,500+ orders/mo, unlimited stores.
- All tiers: unlimited ad pixels, unlimited ad spend/revenue tracking, 24/7 live chat.
- 14-day free trial (no payment required). 60-day money-back guarantee.
**Positioning one-liner:** "The #1 Ad Tracking Software for Shopify & WooCommerce" -- first-party server-side tracking that recovers 60% of lost ad spend and improves ROAS 50% within one week.

## What they do well
- **First-party server-side tracking with data enrichment.** Core technical approach: captures purchase/checkout/cart events directly from Shopify/WooCommerce backend, enriches them with proprietary 360-degree data enrichment engine (session stitching across devices/browsers, even when cookies are blocked), and pushes enriched conversion data server-side to ad platforms in their native API format.
- **Direct ad-platform feedback loop.** Enriched data is sent back to Meta CAPI, Google Ads, TikTok, etc. in the exact format each platform expects, which improves ad algorithm learning and optimization -- not just reporting accuracy.
- **WooCommerce support.** Genuine differentiator vs. Triple Whale, Klar, Lifetimely, and most T1 competitors. Supports WooCommerce alongside multiple checkout plugins.
- **Outstanding customer support.** Consistently the #1 praised feature across 659+ reviews (4.9/5 across G2, Capterra, GetApp). "Real human within 10 minutes, no bots." Support helps with setup and ongoing optimization.
- **Simple, transparent pricing.** Order-based tiers with no hidden add-ons, no modular upsells. $49/mo entry is the cheapest in the attribution category.
- **Ad-blocker proof.** Server-side approach bypasses client-side ad blockers entirely.
- **Klaviyo integration.** Server-side Klaviyo abandoned-cart flows plugin -- unique add-on that extends value beyond pure tracking.

## Weaknesses / common complaints
- **Not an analytics platform.** No dashboards for P&L, LTV, cohorts, retention, or blended metrics. This is a tracking/attribution pipe, not a reporting tool. Users still need a separate analytics layer.
- **Pricing scales with order volume.** Multiple reviewers note costs become expensive for high-volume stores needing advanced features.
- **Facebook Ads Manager data delay.** "The tracking is shown after a long delay on Facebook ads manager" -- enriched data takes time to propagate back to platform dashboards.
- **14-day trial too short.** Reviewers say it is not enough time to see long-term attribution improvements.
- **No mobile app.**
- **Limited reporting customization.** Dashboard is minimal; no custom reports, no export flexibility, no SQL.
- **No MMM or incrementality.** Pure last-touch server-side attribution. No multi-touch modeling, no marketing mix modeling.
