# Klar

**URL:** https://klar.io
**Target customer:** European DTC brands on Shopify, typically <EUR 1.5M to EUR 20M+ annual revenue. Sweet spot is German/EU founder-operators who want GDPR-compliant analytics without sending data to US servers. Also targets agencies managing EU portfolios.
**Pricing:** Revenue-based, billed monthly or annually.
- **Klar Core** -- EUR 200/mo (up to EUR 1.5M annual revenue). All reports, customization, integrations, unlimited users.
- **Klar Core + Attribution** -- EUR 400/mo (up to EUR 1.5M annual revenue). Adds multi-touch attribution, MMM, tracking journeys.
- Price scales upward with annual revenue; exact tiers above EUR 1.5M not published.
- Free to install on Shopify (external billing). 14-day free trial.
**Positioning one-liner:** "The Attribution & Insights Platform for your eCom Business" -- European-first Triple Whale alternative with GDPR compliance and MMM baked in.

## What they do well
- **GDPR-first, data hosted in Germany.** Genuine differentiator for EU brands worried about Schrems II / US data transfers. No other T1 competitor offers this.
- **MMM + multi-touch attribution combined.** MMM redistributes direct & branded traffic back to originating channels using ML models. Dynamic and static attribution models offered alongside tracking journeys -- more methodological range than Triple Whale at the same price point.
- **Fast onboarding.** "Minutes, not months" -- connect Shopify + ad accounts via OAuth, see reports immediately. Minimal setup friction compared to Northbeam or Polar.
- **Profitability + retention in one platform.** Revenue, margin analytics, marketing costs, cohort retention KPIs, LTV, creative analysis, and influencer management all included -- no modular upsells.
- **Clean, focused UI.** Dashboards integrate Shopify, Klaviyo, GA4, and ad account data into unified views. Less cluttered than Triple Whale's widget grid.
- **Affordable entry point.** EUR 200/mo for Core is significantly cheaper than Polar (~EUR 700/mo) or Triple Whale ($179+ USD) at equivalent revenue tiers, especially for sub-EUR 1.5M brands.

## Weaknesses / common complaints
- **Tiny review footprint.** Only 1 review on Shopify App Store (5.0 stars), minimal G2/Capterra presence. Hard to validate claims independently.
- **Shopify-only.** No WooCommerce, BigCommerce, or Magento support surfaced. Same limitation as Triple Whale.
- **Integration breadth is narrower.** Confirmed: Shopify, Meta, Google Ads, TikTok, Klaviyo, GA4. Missing: Pinterest, Snapchat, Amazon, Recharge, Criteo -- connectors Polar and Triple Whale already have.
- **No warehouse-native option.** Unlike Polar (Snowflake) or Daasity, Klar does not expose raw data for SQL access.
- **Incrementality testing is marketing-positioned but details are thin.** MMM is included, but geo-holdout experiments or true incrementality tests (a la SegmentStream / Polar) are not clearly documented.
- **No mobile app.** No evidence of a dedicated mobile experience.
- **Small team, niche market.** Munich-based startup; longevity risk compared to VC-backed competitors.
