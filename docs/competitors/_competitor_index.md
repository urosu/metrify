### Surprising findings during discovery
- **Lebesgue** is more central than expected — it shows up in every comparison list as a "competitor benchmarks + first-party pixel + AI guidance" hybrid that bridges T1 and T3.
- **AdBeacon** is gaining traction as the "flat-rate, no add-ons" alternative to Triple Whale; mentioned in 2026 alternative articles consistently.
- **Cometly** appears more frequently than Northbeam in SMB alternatives lists — it has self-serve traction Northbeam lacks.
- **StoreHero** is a relatively young entrant that several comparison articles position as a clean, contribution-margin-first alternative to BeProfit + Triple Whale combined.
- **Conjura** has reframed itself around "Owly AI" — natural-language analytics is now a major axis among 2026 entrants.
- **Native Shopify Sidekick** is now free across every plan and increasingly cannibalises the "ask my analytics in plain English" wedge that ReportGenix / Lebesgue / Triple Whale charge for.
